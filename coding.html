What is coding?

In the most simple terms, coding is a list of instructions. Anything like the rules for a sports game or instructions on how to make a sandwich could be seen as code. The code that goes into our computer is called programming, it tells the computer what to do. There's a small computer in almost all devices, your phone, your remote, your microwave, each of these things needs code to know what to do. Programmers are the people who tell computers and phones what to do, they get to make games, websites, and even AI.

If you enjoy making things, coding may be perfect for you!
